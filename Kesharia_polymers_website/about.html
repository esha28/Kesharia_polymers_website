About Us

About Company

With a humble inception in 1989, Shri Mukesh Singhai founded the very roots of Kesharia Polymer with a sole aim of manufacturing Best-In-Class PVC Compound for wires and cables industry.


Since then we are engaged in manufacturing and supplying a vast range of high performance PVC Compound such as wire and cable compound, footwear, medical compound, profile compound, railway signaling compound, thermoplastic rubber compound, automotive and EVA Compounds,etc.


It’s been more than 25 years now for Kesharia Polymers to have compounded trust with a true sense of integrity. From self-assembling the compounding extruder machinery in the beginning to procuring the latest German Twin Screw fully automatic extruder with an output of approx. 600 kg/hour and applying the latest quality and technological Testing Advancements in the date, Kesharia Polymers has left no stone un-turned in order to provide its customers the best in class quality PVC compound regardless of the complicated problems or dreadful obstacles.
